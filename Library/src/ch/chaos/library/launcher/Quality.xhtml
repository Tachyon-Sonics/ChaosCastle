Quality

The game has a base resolution of 320 x 240. This setting determines how to scale the graphics to the target resolution.

Scaling is always and integer decomposed into two parts: Inner Scaling and Outer Scaling.

  • Inner Scaling uses vector graphics and high quality xBRZ scaling for a smooth result, but is slower.
  • Outer Scaling always uses nearest-neighbour scaling (which results in "pixelated" graphics), but is faster.

High quality favors Inner Scaling whereas low quality favor Outer Scaling. The resulting Inner Scaling and Outer Scaling values are displayed below the "Quality" and "Size (% of screen)" settings.
