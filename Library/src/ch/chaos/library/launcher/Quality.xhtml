Quality

The game has a base resolution of 320x240. This setting determines how to scale the graphics to the target resolution.

High values mean that most of the scaling will use vector graphics and xBRZ for a smooth, but slower result. Low values mean that most of the scaling will use nearest-neighbour sacling (which results in "pixelated" graphics), but is faster.

See information of the "Resulting Scalings" for more.
