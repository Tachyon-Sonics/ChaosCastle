Resulting Scalings

The resulting scalings shown here are determined by the "Quality" and "Size (% of screen)" settings.

The game uses a base resolution of 320x240. As most screens have a higher resolution, scaling is used.

Scaling is always and integer decomposed into two parts: Inner Scaling and Outer Scaling.

  • Inner Scaling uses vector graphics and high quality xBRZ scaling for a smooth result, but is slower. It is also limited to products of powers of 2, 3 and 5.
  • Outer Scaling always uses nearest-neighbour scaling (which results in "pixelated" graphics), but is faster.
